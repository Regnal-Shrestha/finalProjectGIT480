The Irish Know How To Party!

Fibber Magees, the only truly authentic Irish pub and restaurant in the east valley, has been in business since March 2003. Our mission statement is simple, "To be the highest quality authentic Irish Pub in the market, offering excellent standards of food, drink, guest service and hospitality".Our owners and staff would like to thank everyone who has supported us for over 22 years. It is because of you the craic is mighty. Fibbers is the perfect place to hold your next event or get-together. Please call us or stop by for more information. We hope we will see you soon

The higlights of the day, enjoy

Hunter's Kill

Savory 12 ounce steak cooked to order, smothered in garlic and onion in house specialty sauce. Something that is a poster child for a staple hunters meal that any hunter would love.

Burger

A burger with perfect ratio of lean meat to fat ground beef with a slice of gouda cheese, bacon, pickles and many other addons you can choose from. Comes with a side of fries and your choice of dipping sauce.

Bear Spoils

Cod fish breaded to a golden brown with a side of your choosing. Just like a bear who finally caught a Salmon swimming upstream, you'll know how it feels to have the spoils of a well made meal.
